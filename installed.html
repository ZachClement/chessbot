Introducing

You just installed or updated "Chess.com bot" extension. This automated computer program worked as Google Chrome extension. You can use it when you visit Chess.com, then log in or register new account and play.

How to play

When you start playing new game (online or live mode) you will see a robot icon (position defers on playing mode). Right from them you will see best next move. Chess engine working in background and you will see new suggestion after few moves.

Also since version 0.4.4 it will be pink squares on the board which show you next move. Look how it looks on one of my games (i highlight all two suggestions with red square):

Also you can switch it on or off in anytime you need it. Simple click on robot icon to turn suggestions on and off. After click on them, robot will look like that: and no one suggestion will be showed. In online chess mode it will also gray background for robot icon when suggestion disabled.

Fair playing

Use cheating program to win is illegal. See the rules: http://www.chess.com/legal#rules.

My personal note: please, use my extension only for education not to suppress any other players. Remember: fair play is the best way to play and the only way to train your skill.
